AlifPlay — iOS Privacy Policy

This policy explains how the iOS version handles data in compliance with Apple’s App Store Review Guidelines.

About AlifPlay

AlifPlay helps learners explore the Arabic alphabet via lessons, tracing, sounds, and mini-games. It is designed for children and beginners.

Data We Collect

  • No personal information required.
  • No tracking across apps or websites.
  • On-device progress only (not uploaded).
  • Crash diagnostics (optional) via Apple’s system diagnostics, anonymous and not identifying.

Ads

If ads are enabled, they are non-personalized/contextual and family-friendly.

Device Permissions

  • Audio to play pronunciations and game sounds.
  • Touch input for tracing and gameplay.
  • No location, camera, microphone recording, contacts, or photos.

Children’s Privacy

  • No social integrations or child-accessible external links.
  • No IAP without parental authorization.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Your Choices

  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.
  • Parents can manage content via iOS Screen Time/Restrictions.

Contact

Email: support@alifplay.app
