AlifPlay — Android Privacy Policy

Effective: September 30, 2025

Data Collection & Usage

  • No account or personal information is required.
  • No tracking across apps or websites.
  • Learning progress is stored locally on the device only.

Ads

If enabled, ads are non-personalized/contextual and family-friendly.

Permissions

  • Audio for pronunciations and game sounds.
  • No access to location, camera, contacts, or photos.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share user data with third parties.
